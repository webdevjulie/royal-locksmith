Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our locksmith services

lock How quickly can you respond to emergency calls?

expand_more

We typically respond to emergency lockout calls within 15-30 minutes, depending on your location and traffic conditions. Our technicians are strategically positioned throughout our service areas to ensure rapid response times.

attach_money Do you provide upfront pricing?

expand_more

Yes, we believe in transparent pricing. After assessing your situation, we'll provide you with a clear, upfront quote before beginning any work. No hidden fees or surprise charges.

verified Are you licensed and insured?

expand_more

Absolutely! We hold NJ DCA License #13VH13550300 and are fully bonded and insured. This protects both you and us during all service calls.

construction What types of locks can you install?

expand_more

We install all types of locks including deadbolts, smart locks, high-security locks, commercial grade locks, and specialty security systems. We work with all major brands and can recommend the best solution for your needs.

verified_user Do you offer warranty on your services?

expand_more

Yes, we stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty on both parts and labor. The specific warranty terms depend on the type of service provided, and we'll explain all warranty details before beginning work.
